About Sprack

Sprack is a community-powered coffee shop finder built with love for coffee enthusiasts. Users can discover, favorite, and submit their favorite shops. Admins help verify data to keep the map clean and helpful. Obviously there are or have been other apps like "Sprack," what makes us different is that Sprack is developed by music industry personnel, because Load-in cannot happen without the first Sprack.
